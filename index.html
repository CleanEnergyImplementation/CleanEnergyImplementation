Implementation of Federal Clean Energy Programs: Lessons Learned from DOE and Partner Agencies

Published: October 2025 | Authors: Ramsey Fahs, Alan Propp, Louise White

Read the Report

About This Report

This report distills insights from more than 80 former federal staff and clean energy implementers who led the rollout of the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law and Inflation Reduction Act. It examines why translating historic investments into real-world projects proved so difficult—and what can be done differently next time.

About The Authors

Ramsey Fahs worked at DOE’s Office of Clean Energy Demonstrations & Loan Programs Office.

Alan Propp worked at the DOE’s Loan Programs Office from 2023-25, where he helped implement billions in clean energy financing. Previously, he served as Senior Policy Advisor to the Deputy Mayor for Operations & Infrastructure in Washington, DC, and worked at Delivery Associates as an implementation consultant with cities across the country on the American Cities Climate Challenge. He is currently a Principal at Blue Crab Strategies. Alan holds a B.A. and M.A. from Stanford University and is based in Washington, DC.

Louise White worked at DOE Loan Programs Office and Office of Technology Transitions
